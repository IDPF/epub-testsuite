[REQUIRED] fxl-170fixed format wt PPD=rtl and page4.page-spread-left

Tests that when the fixed format properties are set so that the page progression direction is right to left, and page 4 is set to page-spread-left, all the page renders correctly.

These pages should be displayed so when the user pages from the beginning to the end, he or she pages from the right to the left.

When the reading device is held with a landscape orientation, this current page should be on the right hand side, while the page labelled "Page 3" should be blank and to the left of the contiguous two page spread. Paging to the next page should display "Page 4" on the left, and a blank page on the right of the contiguous two page spread. Continuing to page should display "Page 6" on the left and "Page 5" on the right of the next contiguous two page spread, then "Page 8" on the left and "Page 7" on the right of the next contiguous two page spread, then "Page 10" on the left and "Page 9" on the right of the next contiguous two page spread, then a single page displaying "Page 11" on the right.

When the reading device is held with a portrait orientation, paging from the beginning to the end, that is, the right-most page to the left-most page, after displaying this current page, the device should display single individual pages sequentially numbered from 3 to 11.
