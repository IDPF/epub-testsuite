[REQUIRED] mathml-020 MathML styling

Tests whether basic MathML styling is supported.

2 ⁡ x + y + z

The test passes if the equation "2x+y+z" has a yellow background, a dashed border with padding, the first occurrence of + is enlarged to 150% font size, the identifiers (x, y, z) are colored white on green background. The visual rendering should be similar to the following image.
